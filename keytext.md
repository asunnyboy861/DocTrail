# App Store Metadata

## Promotional Text
Track every version of your documents. Import, compare, and restore any version with side-by-side diff. iCloud sync and team sharing included.

## Description
DocTrail is the version control system your documents have been waiting for. Never lose track of changes again - every edit, every revision, every milestone is captured and organized in a beautiful timeline.

Whether you are managing contracts, design files, reports, or any document that evolves over time, DocTrail gives you complete visibility into what changed, when it changed, and who changed it.

KEY FEATURES

Version Timeline
See the full history of every document at a glance. Each version is timestamped, attributed, and annotated so you always know the story behind your files.

Side-by-Side Diff
Compare any two versions with a detailed change breakdown. Added, modified, deleted, renamed - every difference is highlighted and categorized so nothing slips through.

One-Tap Restore
Need to go back? Restore any previous version instantly. The current version is preserved, so you can always move forward again.

iCloud Sync
Access your document history from all your devices. Changes sync automatically through iCloud so your team always has the latest.

Team Collaboration
Share documents with your team. Lock files during editing, track who made each change, and keep everyone aligned with a shared activity feed.

Smart Organization
Tag documents for quick retrieval. Search by name, tag, or content. Filter by status, owner, or date to find exactly what you need.

Document Locking
Prevent conflicting edits by locking documents while you work. Team members see who is editing and can wait their turn.

Any File Type
DocTrail works with PDFs, Word documents, spreadsheets, presentations, images, and any other file format you use.
